No Mercy Rules

  • Stacking: +2, +4, +6, and +10 cards can be stacked.
  • Mercy Rule: If a player has 25+ cards, they are eliminated!
  • 0 & 7: Play a 0 to pass hands in order; play a 7 to swap hands with anyone.
  • Jump-in: Not active in this version for stability.
